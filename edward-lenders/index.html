Edward - The Profession Planner

The issue The advent of the Digital Economy, ushered in by the nexus of forces that is, Cloud, Big Data, Mobility and Social Business has bought a plethora of new business opportunities but in the midst of this transformation, organizations are facing a huge talent gap. This gap is a result of education programs being insufficient in keeping up with the rapidly changing tech industry.

The solution Edward is the profession planner. Institutional education is no longer sufficient for a professional, to keep up with the changing trends they must constantly under go micro-learning programs. These programs are numerous and one may not know which of them are relevant for their careers. So Edward steps in and plans a learning map as well as a cost structure for these programs.
